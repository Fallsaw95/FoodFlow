Espresso

A rich and concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Cappuccino

A popular Italian coffee drink that combines espresso, steamed milk, and a layer of frothed milk.

Latte

A coffee drink made with espresso and a larger amount of steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam.

Mocha

A blend of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate, often topped with whipped cream.

Macchiato

An espresso with a small amount of milk or milk foam, creating a "stained" or "marked" coffee.

Americano

A diluted espresso with added hot water, resulting in a milder flavor compared to straight espresso.

Flat White

A coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, similar to a latte but with a different milk-to-espresso ratio.

Iced Coffee

Coffee brewed normally and then cooled, served over ice. Often sweetened and with milk or cream.

Cold Brew

Coffee brewed with cold water over an extended period, resulting in a smoother and less acidic taste.

Turkish Coffee

A traditional method of coffee preparation where very finely ground coffee is simmered with water and often sugar.
